Relation: has-goal

From: Entity

To: Event

Description:

The goal of an Entity.

Examples:

  1. The goal of every participant at the Olympics is to win the gold medal.
  2. Sam wants to get a second job in order to make more money.

Neighboring Concepts:

Similar to...But different because...
objective has-goal expresses the goals of an Entity. The Entity will ususally perform some Intentional Actions whose objectives will include its goals.
