Concept: Allocate-Resource

Superconcept: Action

Component Semantics:

Allocate-Resource
Definition: allocates an object that is playing the Role of a Resource, thus making it unavailable to the original base.

Required Relationships:

object: the Entity playing the Role of the Resource.
agent: the Entity that makes the Resource unavailable.
base: what the Resource is made unavailable to.
