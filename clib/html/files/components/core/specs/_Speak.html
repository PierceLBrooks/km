Concept: Speak

Superconcept: Embody

Definition: The physical act of a producing speech sounds.

Required Relationships:

object: Message being converted to sound.
result: the Sound that embodies the message

Examples:

  1. Fred spoke the news.
    agent: Fred
    object: the news (qua Message encoded in a language).
    result: sound waves that Embody the message.
  2. Polly the parrot squawked "Hello".
    agent: Polly
    object: the Message "Hello".
    result: sound waves that Embody the message.
