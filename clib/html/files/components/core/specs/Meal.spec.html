Concept: Meal

Superconcept: Activity

Component Semantics:

Meal
Definition: the Activity of eating. It can be elaborated upon, but there is at least Eating going on.

Required Relationships:

sub-event: Must have a sub-event of Eating.

Neighboring Concepts:

Similar to...But different because...
Breakfast Breakfast is a special case of Meal - the Activity of eating (usually) early in the morning.
Brunch Brunch is a special case of Meal - the Activity of eating (usually) late in the morning - as a combination of Breakfast and Lunch.
Lunch Lunch is a special case of Meal - the Activity of eating (usually) at mid-day.
Dinner Dinner is a special case of Meal - the Activity of eating (usually) in the (late) evening.
Supper Supper is a special case of Meal - the Activity of (light) eating (usually) in the (early) evening.
