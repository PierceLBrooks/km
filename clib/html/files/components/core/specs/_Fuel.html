Concept: Fuel

How to represent roles

Superconcept: Resource

Definition: A Fuel is the Role played by an entity which is the object of a Burn'ing event. The objective of the event is to Heat a nearby Tangible-Entity, which is the object of the Heating.

Required Relationships:

played-by: the entity that's playing the role of Fuel
in-event: the Burn'ing event in which the entity is the Fuel

Examples:

  1. Coal fueled the fire.
    Comment: In this example, the Fuel role is described with these properties:
    in-event: Burn with object=Coal
    played-by: the coal
