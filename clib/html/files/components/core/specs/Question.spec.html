Concept: Question

Superconcept: Role

Definition: Question is the Role played by Information which is the object of a Inquire.

Required Relationships:

played-by: the Information that's playing the role of Question during the Inquire.
in-event: the Inquire event that the Question is expressed during

Examples:

  1. The Philosophy major asked, "Do you want fries with that?"
    Comment: In this example, the role of Question is described with these properties:
    in-event: Inquire=ask with object= "Do you want fries with that?"
    played-by: "Do you want fries with that?"
