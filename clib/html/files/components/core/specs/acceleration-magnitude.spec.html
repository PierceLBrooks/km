Property: acceleration-magnitude

How to represent properties

From: Event

From: Acceleration-Vector-Value

To: Acceleration-Magnitude-Value

Description:

The magnitude of acceleration for a vector. It can take both negative and positive values.

Value Description:

Numeric Values:

a number along with any of the units of measurement for UoM-Acceleration

  • The car travelled at a speed of 40 m/s/s
    number: 40
    unit of measure: m/s^2
