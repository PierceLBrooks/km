Concept: Region

Superconcept: Place

Component Semantics:

Region
Definition: a Place with spatial extent, typically a subregion of some Tangible-Entity.

Other Common Relationships:

is-region-of: The Tangible-Entity on which the Region is a subregion
has-region: The relation that the Tangible-Entity has to the Region

Examples:

  1. There is a heart-shaped birthmark on a dime-sized Region on her leg.
    is-region-of: her leg
    is-location-of: a heart-shaped birthmark

Neighboring Concepts:

Similar to...But different because...
Place Place is a more general form for Region - it does not specify any spatial extent.
Territory Territory is a special case of Region - alongwith spatial extent, it also specifies the reason for the demarcation.
