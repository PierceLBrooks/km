Concept: Teach

Superconcept: Inform

Definition: A Teacher informs a Pupil of Information not previously known by the pupil.

Required Relationships:

agent: the Tangible-Entity that plays the role of Teacher
recipient: the Tangible-Entity that plays the role of Pupil. It's assumed that the pupil doesn't know the information before the event occurs
object: the Information that's learned by the recipient

Examples:

  1. Tom taught Joe that Earth is a planet.
    agent: Tom
    recipient: Joe
    object: Earth is a planet

    2. Neighboring Concepts:

    Similar to...But different because...
    Learn Learn involves an agent acquiring new knowledge, possibly without the help of a teacher
    Lie Lie involves expressing false information
    Deceive Deceive is a lie that is believed by the recipient
