Concept: Information

Superconcept: Intangible-Entity

Definition: The knowledge communicated or received concerning a particular fact or circumstance.

Required Relationships:

information-content: knowledge being communicated

Other Common Relationships:

truth: whether the information-content is true or false

Examples:

  1. The teacher taught us about World War II.
    Comment: In this example, the Information is described by these properties:
    information-content: Word War II
  2. People used to believe that the earth was flat.
    Comment: In this example, the Information is described by these properties:
    information-content: the earth was flat
    truth: false

Neighboring Concepts:

Similar to...But different because...
Language The form in which the information is written out.
