Concept: Eat

Superconcept: Take-In

Component Semantics:

Eat
Definition: take in (solid) food; used of animals only.

Required Relationships:

object: the Tangible-Entity that is playing the Role of Food that is taken in.
agent: the Animal that is taking in the Food.

Examples:

  1. The squirrel eats the nut.
    agent: the squirrel
    object: the nut
