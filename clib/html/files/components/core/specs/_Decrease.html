Concept: Decrease

Superconcept: Action

Definition: A property of an Entity or Event decreases

Required Relationships:

from-value: the initial value of the property-slot before the decrease
to-value: the final value of the property-slot after the decrease

Examples:

  1. The water in the tank dropped from 20 to 10 gallons.
    base: The water in the tank
    from-value: 20 gallons
    to-value: 10 gallons
  2. John cooled the broth from boiling to 55F.
    agent: John
    base: the broth
    from-value: boiling
    to-value: 55F
