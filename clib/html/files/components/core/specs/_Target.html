Concept: Target

How to represent roles

Superconcept: Role

Definition: A Target is the Entity that is the destination in a Move.

Required Relationships:

played-by: the Entity that plays the Target
in-event: the Move that the Entity participates in

Examples:

  1. The ball moved towards the sideline.
    Comment: In this example, the Role is Target, with these properties:
    in-event: move
    played-by: the sideline
  2. The car pulls into a gas station.
    Comment: In this example, the Role is Target, with these properties:
    in-event: pulls into
    played-by: gas station
