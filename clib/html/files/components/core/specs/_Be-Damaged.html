Concept: Be-Damaged

Superconcept: State

Definition: An object becomes weakened or less effective in some way. The object can still perform its function. An object is put in this state by Damage.

Required Relationships:

object: the thing that becomes damaged
Restriction: this must be a Tangible-Entity

Examples:

  1. The TV was damaged.
    object: the TV
  2. The man scratched his watch.
    object: his watch

Neighboring Concepts:

Similar to...But different because...
Be-Broken Be-Broken is for things that are damaged to the point where they are no longer able to perform their intended functions.
