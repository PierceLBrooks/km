Concept: Authorize

Superconcept: Action

Authorize
Definition: The Action of granting authorization

Required Relationships:

agent: the Entity who authorizes.
is-goal-of: the Entity whose goal is the authorization.

Other Common Relationships:

enables: the State that is enabled as a result.

Examples:

  1. John authorized Jane to sell the car.
    Comment: This is represented as a Authorize with the following properties:
    agent: John
    is-goal-of: John
    enables: jane to sell the car
