Concept: Agent-Role

How to represent roles

Superconcept: Role

Definition: The Role played by the entity that initiates, performs, or causes an event

Required Relationships:

played-by: the Entity playing the role of agent
in-event: the Event in which the entity is playing that role

Examples:

  1. A passenger had to be the pilot after the pilot died during the flight
    in-event: fly
    played-by: passenger
    2. Comment: The Agent-Role might be called "pilot"
  2. John hammered the nail.
    in-event: hammer the nail
    played-by: John
    Comment: The Agent-Role might be called "hammerer"

Neighboring Concepts:

Similar to...But different because...
Donor-Role The Role played by the entity that relinquishes possession of an object during a Transfer
Recipient-Role The Role played by the entity that gains possession of an object during a Transfer
Instrument-Role The role played by things, such as tools, used by an agent in an Event
