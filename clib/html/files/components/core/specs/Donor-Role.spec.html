Concept: Donor-Role

How to represent roles

Superconcept: Role

Definition: A Donor (here called a "Donor-Role") is the Role played by the donor of a Transfer.

Required Relationships:

played-by: the entity that's playing the role of Donor
in-event: the Transfer event that the entity is engaged in

Examples:

  1. Fred donated stock certificates to GoodWill.
    Comment: In this example, the Donor-Role is described with these properties:
    in-event: Transfer with object=stock certificates and recipient=GoodWill
    played-by: Fred

Neighboring Concepts:

Similar to...But different because...
Recipient-Role A Recipient-Role is played by an Entity on the receiving end of a Transfer.
