Concept: Hear

Superconcept: Sense

Definition: Sensing a sound auditorily.

Required Relationships:

experiencer: The Tangible-Entity doing the hearing
object: The vibrating Tangible-Entity (e.g. air) playing the role of Signal.
result: The Message received by the experiencer.
instrument: The sensory apparatus and any amplifiers (e.g. hearing aids) used in a given Hear event.

Examples:

  1. Fred heard the ring of his cell phone.
    experiencer: Fred
    object: vibrating air acting as Fred's cell phone ringing sound
    result: a Message to Fred that he is being phoned.
