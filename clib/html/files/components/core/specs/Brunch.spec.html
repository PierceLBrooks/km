Concept: Brunch

Superconcept: Meal

Component Semantics:

Brunch
Definition: the Activity of eating (usually) late in the morning - as a combination of Breakfast and Lunch.

Neighboring Concepts:

Similar to...But different because...
Meal Meal is the general Activity involving eating - Brunch is a special case.
Breakfast Breakfast is also a special case of Meal, but is the Activity of eating (usually) early in the morning.
Lunch Lunch is also a special case of Meal, but is the Activity of eating (usually) at mid-day.
Dinner Dinner is also a special case of Meal, but is the Activity of eating (usually) in the (late) evening.
Supper Supper is also a special case of Meal, but is the Activity of (light) eating (usually) in the (early) evening.
