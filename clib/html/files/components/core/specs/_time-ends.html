Relation: time-ends

From: Temporal-Entity

To: Time-Instant

Description:

The time instant at which a time interval ends. NOTE: The time-ends of a time instant is defined as the instant itself.

Examples:

  1. The football game (November 24th, 2006, 4pm through 8pm) ends at 8pm on Nov 24th, 2006
  2. The final exams (November 22nd, 2006 through December 7th, 2006) end on the first thursday of december 2006.
