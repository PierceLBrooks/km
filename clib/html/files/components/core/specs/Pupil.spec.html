Concept: Pupil

Superconcept: Role

Definition: A pupil is the Role played by a Tangible-Entity that is the recipient of Teaching

Required Relationships:

played-by: the Tangible-Entity playing the role of Pupil
in-event: the Teach event in which the pupil is the recipient

Examples:

  1. Yoda taught Luke.
    Comment: In this example, the role of Pupil is described with these properties:
    in-event: Teach with agent=Yoda and recipient=Luke
    played-by: Luke
