Concept: Transmit

Superconcept: Move

Definition: A Signal moves to one or more recipients.
Comment: Transmit is the second step of Convey (which itself is a step of Communicate). For perspective, here's the overall model of convey. First, a Message is Embody'ed as a Tangible-Entity. Second, that entity, playing the role of a Signal, is Transmited to the recipients. Finally, the signal is Sensed by the recipient and converted back into a message.

Required Relationships:

object: the Tangible-Entity, playing the role of a Signal, that is transmitted

Other Common Relationships:

agent: the Tangible-Entity doing the transmitting
recipient: the Tangible-Entity receiving the signal. The location of the recipient is assumed to be the destination of the transmission.
instrument: the Tangible-Entity, playing the role of Carrier for the signal
base: the Tangible-Entity, playing the role of Medium through which the signal moves
Comment: See also the "Common Relationships" used to describe any Move
