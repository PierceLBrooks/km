Relation: recipient

From: Event

To: Entity

Description:

The recipient of an event is the entity that receives (takes possession of) the object of the event.

Examples:

  1. The orphans received some donations.
  2. The alumni gave more money to the library.
  3. She stole my heart and my cat.

Neighboring Concepts:

Similar to...But different because...
beneficiary A beneficiary benefits from an event, even though there may be no transfer of any object.
