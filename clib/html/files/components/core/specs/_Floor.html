Concept: Floor

Superconcept: Platform

Definition: A Structure that is part of a Tangible-Entity playing Container; everything on it is content of the Container.

Other Common Relationships:

is-part-of: the Tangible-Entity that plays a Container, that the Floor is part of.

Examples:

  1. bedroom floor
    is-part of: bedroom

Neighboring Concepts:

A Building is a Structure that contains Persons, Tangible-Entities, Walls, a Roof, Floors, Rooms, Portals, Hallways and plaus the Role of a Container.
Similar to...But different because...
Room A Structure within a Building; has Walls, Floor, Ceiling, Portal(s) and plays the Role of a Container
Wall A vertical Structure, playing the Role of a Barrier
Ceiling A surface, part of a Room, plays the Role of Cover for a Room, is-above everything in room.
Platform Horizontal Structure, supports everything on it, is-below everything on it.
Roof A Structure that plays Cover for another Structure; if the second Structure plays a Container, then the first is is-above the content of the second.
