Concept: Waste

How to represent roles

Superconcept: Role

Definition: Waste is the Role played by a Tangible-Entity which is the waste product of a Consume event.

Required Relationships:

played-by: the entity that's playing the role of Waste
in-event: the Consume event that produces the waste product

Examples:

  1. Black soot was produced by the burning (consuming) of coal.
    Comment: In this example, the waste product is described with these properties:
    in-event: Burn with object=Coal
    played-by: the black soot
