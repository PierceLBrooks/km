Concept: Room

Superconcept: Structure

Definition: A Structure within a Building, that has walls, floor, ceiling, portal, and plays Container.

Other Common Relationships:

is-part-of: the Building that the Room is part of

Examples:

  1. hotel room
    is-part-of: hotel

Neighboring Concepts:

Similar to...But different because...
Bridge A Structure that plays the Role of a Conduit over something playing the Role of a Barrier
Building A Building is a Structure that contains Persons, Tangible-Entities, Walls, a Roof, Floors, Rooms, Portals, Hallways and plaus the Role of a Container.
Wall A vertical Structure, playing the Role of a Barrier
Ceiling A surface, part of a Room, plays the Role of Cover for a Room, is-above everything in room.
Platform Horizontal Structure, supports everything on it, is-below everything on it.
Roof A Structure that plays Cover for another Structure; if the second Structure plays a Container, then the first is is-above the content of the second.
