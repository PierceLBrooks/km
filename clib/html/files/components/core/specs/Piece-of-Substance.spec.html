Concept: Piece-of-Substance

Superconcept: Inanimate-Object

Definition: An Inanimate-Object without any macro parts, made out of only one material

Required Relationships:

material: The Substance the object is made of

Examples:

  1. A batholith is an underground rock formation uncovered by erosion.
    Comment: In this example, the batholith is described with the following properties:
    instance-of: Piece-of-Substance with materialstone
