Property: temperature

How to represent properties

From: Tangible-Entity

To: Temperature-Value

Description:

the degree of hotness or coldness of a body or environment

Value Description:

Numeric Values:

a number along with any of the units of measurement for UoM-Temperature

  • 112.2 degrees celsius
    number: 112.2
    unit of measure: *degrees-celsius

    • Relative Values:

    a relative value along with a reference concept (what is the relative value relative to?)

    values: cold, cool, mild, warm, hot
    *cold-hot-scale: cold..cool..mild..warm..hot
    values: freezing, boiling, normal, abnormal
    *freezing-boiling-scale: freezing..boiling..normal..abnormal

  • my tea is hot relative to Sodas.
    value: hot
    relative to: Soda
