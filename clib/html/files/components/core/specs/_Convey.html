Concept: Convey

Superconcept: Action

Definition: An agent conveys a Message to a recipient.
Comment: Convey is the second step of Communicate. For perspective, here's the overall model of communication. Information is first converted into a message by the Express subevent, then the message is conveyed to the recipient by this event, and finally the message is converted back into information by the Interpret subevent.

Required Relationships:

object: the Message being conveyed

Other Common Relationships:

agent: the Tangible-Entity that conveys the message
recipient: the Tangible-Entity that receives the message
base: the Tangible-Entity, playing the role of a Signal, that is the embodiment of the message

Subevents:

Embody: the message is embodied as a signal
Transmit: the signal is transmitted to the recipient
Sense: the recipient senses the signal

Subevent Order:

Embody, followed by Transmit, followed by Sense.
