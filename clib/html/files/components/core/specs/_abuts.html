Relation: abuts

From: Spatial-Entity

To: Spatial-Entity

Description:

A thing or place is adjacent to another.

Examples:

  1. The tennis court is next to the basketball court.
    2. Comment: Here "is next to" is translated to "abuts" between the tennis and basketball court.
  2. Canada adjoins the U.S.
  3. The parking is adjacent to the shopping center
