Concept: Replenish

Superconcept: Increase

Definition: An increase in the amount of some Entity which is playing the role of a Resource

Required Relationships:

object: the entity replenished
base: the Resource that the entity is playing
property-slot: the property of the object that increases

Other Common Relationships:

from-value: the initial value of the property-slot before the replenishment
to-value: the final value of the property-slot after the replenishment

Examples:

  1. John's car runs on ethanol. He re-filled the tank.
    object: the ethanol in the tank
    base: the fuel (a resource) used in John's engine's operation
    property-slot: volume
    to-value: full
