Concept: Connector

How to represent roles

Superconcept: Role

Definition: Connector is the Role played by an Entity which is the instrument of a Be-Attached-To.

Required Relationships:

played-by: the entity that's playing the role of Connector
in-event: the Be-Attached-To event that the Entity is engaged in

Examples:

  1. The bike is connected to the rack with a chain.
    Comment: In this example, the Connector role is described with these properties:
    in-event: Be-Attached-To
    played-by: chain
