Relation: time-int-during

From: Time-Interval

To: Time-Interval

Description:

A time interval occurs during another, bigger time interval. More specifically, (a) The start time of one interval is greater than or equal to the start time of another AND (b) The end time of the first interval is lesser than or equal to the end time of the other.

Examples:

  1. the half time break was taken from 5pm to 5:30pm during the game, which was played from 3pm to 7:30pm
