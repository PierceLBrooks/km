Concept: Increase

Superconcept: Action

Definition: A property of an Entity or Event increases

Required Relationships:

from-value: the initial value of the Property that increases
to-value: the final value of the Property that increases

Examples:

  1. The water level rose from 10 to 20 inches.
    base: waterr level
    from-value: 10 inches
    to-value: 20 inches
  2. John heated the broth from freezing to 110F.
    agent: John
    base: the broth
    from-value: freezing
    to-value: 110F
