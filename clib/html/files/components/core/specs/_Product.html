Concept: Product

How to represent roles

Superconcept: Role

Definition: A Product is the Role played by an Entity which is the result of a Produce.

Required Relationships:

played-by: the entity that's playing the role of a Product
in-event: the Produce event that creates the entity

Examples:

  1. The factory produces a car from sheet metal.
    Comment: In this example, the Product role is described with these properties:
    in-event: Produce with raw-material=sheet metal
    played-by: a car
