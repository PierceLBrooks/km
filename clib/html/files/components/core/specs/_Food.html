Concept: Food

How to represent roles

Superconcept: Role

Definition: Food is the Role played by an entity which is the object of a Feed'ing event.

Required Relationships:

played-by: the entity that's playing the role of food
in-event: the Feed'ing event in which the entity is eaten

Examples:

  1. A bird ate the locust.
    Comment: In this example, the Food role is described with these properties:
    in-event: Feed with agent=bird
    played-by: the locust
