Concept: Signal

Superconcept: Role

Definition: Signal is the Role played by Entity which is the object of a Transmit.

Required Relationships:

played-by: the Entity that's playing the role of Signal during the Transmit
in-event: the Transmit event that the Signal is being sent

Examples:

  1. "Mr. Watson come here" was the first message transmitted over a phone line.
    Comment: In this example, the role of Signal is described with these properties:
    in-event: Transmit with object="Mr. Watson come here"
    played-by: "Mr. Watson come here"
