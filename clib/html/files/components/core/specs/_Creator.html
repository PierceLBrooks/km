Concept: Creator

How to represent roles

Superconcept: Role

Definition: A Creator is the Role played by an entity which is the agent of a Create.

Required Relationships:

played-by: the entity that's playing the role of creator
in-event: the Create event that the entity is engaged in

Examples:

  1. The artist created a sculpture.
    Comment: In this example, the role of Creator is described with these properties:
    in-event: Create with object=sculpture
    played-by: the artist
