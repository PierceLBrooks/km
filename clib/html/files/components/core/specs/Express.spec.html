Concept: Express

Superconcept: Action

Definition: An agent expresses Information.
Comment: Express is the first step of Communicate. For perspective, here's the overall model of communication. Information is first converted into a message by the Express subevent, then the message is sent to the recipient by the Convey event, and finally the message is converted back into information by the Interpret subevent.

Required Relationships:

agent: the entity expressing the information
object: the information being expressed

Other Common Relationships:

result: the Message that expresses the information

Examples:

  1. Larry told Sally how he felt.
    agent: Larry
    object: Larry's feelings
    recipient: Sally
  2. The client sent a request to the server.
    agent: the client
    object: the request
    recipient: the server

Neighboring Concepts:

Similar to...But different because...
Command An express in which the information is an Instruction
Inform An express in which the recipient is required
Communicate Communicate is the transfer of information between agents, such that the recipient knows the information afterwards. Express is the first step of a Communicate, and it is followed by Convey and Interpret.
