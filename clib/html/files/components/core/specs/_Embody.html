Concept: Embody

Superconcept: Create

Definition: A Tangible-Entity is created as the physical embodiment of a Message.
Comment: Embody is the first step of Convey (which itself is a step of Communicate). For perspective, here's the overall model of convey. First, a Message is embodied as a Tangible-Entity. Second, that entity, playing the role of a Signal, is Transmited to a recipient. Finally, the signal is Sensed by the recipient and converted back into a message. The Language of the communication is a feature of the Message.

Required Relationships:

object: Message being embodied
result: the Tangible-Entity that embodies the message
