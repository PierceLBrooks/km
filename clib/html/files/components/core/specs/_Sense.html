Concept: Sense

Superconcept: Action

Definition: The experiencer derives a Message from a Sensation using a sensory apparatus
Comment: Sense is the third and final step of Convey (which itself is a step of Communicate). For perspective, here's the overall model of convey. First, a Message is Embody'ed as a Tangible-Entity. Second, that entity, playing the role of a Signal, is Transmited to the recipients. Finally, the signal is sensed by the recipient and converted back into a message.

Required Relationships:

experiencer: The Tangible-Entity doing the sensing
object: The Sensation being sensed
result: The Message received by the experiencer
instrument: The sensory apparatus used in the sensing

Examples:

  1. Jane heard a loud *bang*.
    object: sound wave
    experiencer: Jane
    message: something big is happening
    instrument: ears

Neighboring Concepts:
