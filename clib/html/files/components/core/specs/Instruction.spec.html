Concept: Instruction

Superconcept: Role

Definition: Instruction is the Role played by Information that is the object of a Command

Required Relationships:

played-by: the Information playing the role of Instruction
in-event: the Command event during which the instruction is expressed

Examples:

  1. James asked the waiter to bring him a glass of water.
    Comment: In this example, the role of Instruction is described with these properties:
    in-event: A Command with object="bring James a glass of water" and recipient=the waiter
    played-by: "bring James a glass of water"
