Concept: Recipient-Role

How to represent roles

Superconcept: Role

Definition: A Recipient (here called a "Recipient-Role") is the Role played by the recipient of a Transfer.

Required Relationships:

played-by: the entity that's playing the role of Recipient
in-event: the Transfer event that the entity is engaged in

Examples:

  1. PBS receives contributions from the Carnegie Foundation.
    Comment: In this example, the Recipient-Role is described with these properties:
    in-event: Transfer with object=contributions and donor=Carnegie Foundation
    played-by: PBS

Neighboring Concepts:

Similar to...But different because...
Donor-Role A Donor-Role is played by an Entity that donates something in a Transfer event.
