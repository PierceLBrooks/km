Concept: Vehicle

How to represent roles

Superconcept: Role

Definition: A Vehicle is the Role played by an entity which is the instrument of a Carry'ing event.

Required Relationships:

played-by: the entity that's playing the role of Vehicle
in-event: the Carry'ing event in which the entity is the Vehicle

Examples:

  1. John rode in the wagon.
    Comment: In this example, the Vehicle role is described with these properties:
    in-event: Carry with object=John
    played-by: the wagon
