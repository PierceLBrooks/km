Concept: Place-Order

Superconcept: Action

Definition: To order an Entity from another entity who is the seller.

Required Relationships:

object: The entity that is ordered.
base: The seller who possesses the object.

Other Common Relationships:

agent: The entity that causes the order to occur.
instrument: The entity used to perform the order.

Examples:

  1. The man ordered a computer from the store.
    agent: the man
    object: a computer
    base: the store
