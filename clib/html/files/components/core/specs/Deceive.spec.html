Concept: Deceive

Superconcept: Lie

Definition: Expression of false information that is Interpreted by the recipient .

Required Relationships:

object: the false information that is expressed by the agent and Interpreted by the recipient .
agent: The agent expresses the false information in the object .
recipient: the entity that the deception is expressed to and interpeted by.

Other Common Relationships:

Examples:

  1. Mick decieved his brother to think the, "The sky is green".
    agent: Mick
    object: "the sky is green"
    recipient: Mick's brother

Neighboring Concepts:

Similar to...But different because...
Express Lie is a special type of Express, but Express does not require the Information being expressed have a Truth-Value of false.
Lie Deceive is a special type of Lie in which a recipient is mandatory and that the recipient Interprets the object of the Deceive.
