Concept: Political-State

Superconcept: Administrative-District

Component Semantics:

Political-State
Definition: the territory occupied by one of the constituent administrative districts, specifically called a state, of a nation (Country). A State usually has the second level of government in a Country.

Examples:

  1. Texas is the second-largest state in the USA in area, after Alaska.
    2. Comment: Here "Texas" and "Alaska" are both States.
