Relation: time-before

From: Temporal-Entity

To: Temporal-Entity

Description:

A time period (or time instant) occurs before another time period (or time instant). The first time period ends before the second period (or instant) occurs.

Examples:

  1. I have submitted the paper at an hour before the deadline.
  2. At the university, the beginning of the fall semester, August 29th, 2007, 9am was before the payment deadline for tuition and fees, September 4th, 2007, 5pm.
