Relation: time-int-overlaps

From: Time-Interval

To: Time-Interval

Description:

One time interval overlaps with another. More specifically, start time of an interval is (a) Greater than or equal to that of another AND (b) Lesser than or equal to the end time of the other.

Examples:

  1. Beethoven (1770-1827) and Joseph Haydn (1732-1809) were contemporaries.
  2. John played tennis, from 5 to 6pm while Tom was watching TV, from 5:30 to 6:30pm
