Concept: Teacher

Superconcept: Role

Definition: A teacher is the Role played by a Tangible-Entity that is the agent of Teaching

Required Relationships:

played-by: the Tangible-Entity playing the role of Teacher
in-event: the Teach event in which the teacher is the agent

Examples:

  1. Plato taught Aristotle.
    Comment: In this example, the role of Teacher is described with these properties:
    in-event: Teach with agent=Plato and recipient=Aristotle
    played-by: Plato
