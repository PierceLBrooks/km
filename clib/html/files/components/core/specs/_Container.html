Concept: Container

How to represent roles

Superconcept: Barrier

Definition: Container is the Role played by an Entity which is the instrument of a Be-Contained event.

Required Relationships:

played-by: the entity that's playing the role of container.
in-event: the Be-Contained event that the entity is engaged in
encloses: the Spatial-Entity(s) that is inside the container
does-not-enclose: the Spatial-Entity(s) that is outside the container

Other Common Relationships:

content: the Entity(s) that are contained by the container
capacity: the capacity of the container, expressed as a Capacity-Value

Examples:

  1. a cell contains cytoplasm.
    Comment: In this example, the Container role is described with these properties:
    in-event: Be-Contained with object=cytoplasm
    played-by: cell
